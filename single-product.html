These letter-sized document cases are the perfect solution for holding, protecting and organizing letter-sized papers and documents without hole-punching or folding. Contrasting colored sides and sewn black trim add softness and flexibility. A fabric gusset expands to hold up to 500 sheets, and the elastic closure makes sure they stay securely inside.
