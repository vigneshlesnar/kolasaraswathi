Our Mission

To impart effective communication skill based knowledge in students to face the challenge of the modern world, to be one among the top ten CBSE schools as per the India Today ranking parameter by the year 2018.

Our Vision

The students of Kola Saraswathi Vaishnav Senior Secondary School should be the resource and the treasure of our nation, capable to meet the challenges thrown by the globalization. We commit to facilitate value and skill based, integrated education, to inculcate a sound physical, intellectual, emotional, social and spiritual character in our students so as to shape the destiny of mankind.
